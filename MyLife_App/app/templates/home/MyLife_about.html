About MyLife

MyLife started as a simple idea: what if one app could replace the five you use to manage your day?

The problem we set out to solve

Most people manage their lives across a dozen disconnected tools — a to-do app here, a spreadsheet for finances, a fitness tracker on their phone, a calendar somewhere else. Nothing talks to anything. You spend more time managing your tools than actually living.

MyLife brings it all together in one clean, focused system that mirrors how real life works: your tasks connect to your calendar, your spending connects to your budgets, your fitness connects to your goals.

🎯

Intentional

🔒
